# Target Venue Research

> For pilot onboarding. Last updated: 2026-06-29.

## Research goals

1. Identify 3–5 candidate venues for the PickleBallers pilot program
2. Understand their current booking system (if any)
3. Determine membership needs and pricing models
4. Establish contact and demo timeline

## Candidate profile

Ideal pilot venue:
- 2–6 pickleball courts (dedicated or shared)
- Currently using manual booking (phone, Messenger, pen-and-paper) —OR— a competing platform they're unhappy with
- Has regular players who would benefit from membership tracking
- Located in Metro Manila or nearby (accessible for in-person demos and support)
- Owner is tech-friendly or has staff who can operate the console

## Research questions to ask

From PDF §12 (Open Questions):

1. **Anong system ang gamit nila ngayon para mag-book ng courts?**
   - Manual (tawag/chat/text)? May existing app/platform?
   - Ano ang pinaka-masakit sa current system nila?

2. **Kailangan ba nila ng membership tracking?**
   - May existing membership program ba?
   - Anong klaseng membership — monthly subscription, per-session discount, loyalty points?
   - Ilang active members meron sila?

3. **Ano ang pricing structure nila?**
   - Flat rate per hour? Iba-iba per court/day/time?
   - May weekend/holiday surcharge?
   - May equipment rental?

4. **Paano sila nagre-reconcile ng payments ngayon?**
   - Sino ang nagma-manage ng pera?
   - May official receipts ba sila (BIR)?

5. **Sino ang mag-o-operate ng console day-to-day?**
   - Ang owner mismo o may staff?
   - Ilang tao ang kailangan ng access?

## Open questions (from PDF §12)

1. Can venues use their existing BIR-registered OR numbering, or do they need to adopt PickleBallers' per-venue auto-numbering?
2. For venues that do NOT want member pricing, is the membership feature simply hidden, or is there a way to disable it entirely?
