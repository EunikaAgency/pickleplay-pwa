# ReClub Theming

## Theme Summary

ReClub uses a bright, community-led, mobile-first visual system with:

- warm yellow as a major onboarding and brand surface
- vivid blue-purple for interactive emphasis
- soft light-gray backgrounds
- colorful creation cards
- simple black iconography

The app feels social, friendly, and creator-focused rather than formal or enterprise.

## Visual Character

- Onboarding uses large warm yellow fields with bold black text.
- The app relies on white cards and pale blue-gray page backgrounds.
- Creation choices use large colored panels to distinguish object types.
- Rankings and search overlays use simple line icons with bold text.

## Component Style

### Cards

- large soft radius
- flat or lightly elevated
- lots of whitespace

### Buttons

- blue filled primary buttons
- blue outlined secondary buttons
- black icon accents

### Search and tabs

- wide rounded overlays
- tab labels with blue active underline
- floating search action button appears in several screens

### Lists

- simple stacked rows
- large text
- icon-led metadata

## Typography Direction

The product looks like a geometric rounded sans with bold weights for titles.

Good fit:

- `Poppins`
- `Nunito Sans`
- `Avenir Next`

## Product Mood

- community-first
- bright
- optimistic
- easy to organize with

## Usage Advice

If extending this app, preserve:

- the yellow-and-blue brand contrast
- the colorful creation cards
- the light, open list layouts
- the approachable social tone
