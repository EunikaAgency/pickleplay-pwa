# ReClub Project Overview

## Product Summary

ReClub appears to be a community-first pickleball platform that combines:

- social feed
- club directories and membership spaces
- meet and competition discovery
- creation tools for clubs, meets, and competitions
- player rankings and statistics

Compared with the other captured apps, ReClub looks the most community-native and creation-oriented. It is less focused on traditional court booking and more focused on organizing, joining, and participating in community-led sports activity.

## Main User Types

### Player

Uses the app to:

- browse clubs, meets, competitions, players, and coaches
- join communities
- view rankings and stats
- follow feed updates
- inspect player profiles

### Club member

Uses the app to:

- access club activity, discussion, library, and chat
- view members
- manage club spaces

### Organizer

Uses the app to:

- create a club
- create a meet
- create a competition
- invite friends
- set visibility and sports

## Navigation Model

The root screen appears to be a tile-based dashboard rather than a conventional bottom-tab system.

Main home tiles visible:

```text
My clubs | My network | My history
Feed | Statistics | Settings
Help | Create | See less
```

A global search overlay then acts as a second navigation layer for clubs, meets, competitions, players, and coaches.

## Strong Product Themes

- community over commerce
- creation over pure consumption
- pickleball-specific social infrastructure
- clubs as durable community hubs
- public discovery plus private/member spaces
- rankings and reputation as identity features

## Likely Backend Domains

- auth and onboarding
- club/community entities
- member roles and permissions
- meets and competition objects
- invitations
- player profiles and ratings
- statistics and leaderboards
- notifications and inbox/chat
- coaches and player discovery
- media/feed posts

## Gaps Or Uncaptured Areas

- no conventional court booking checkout
- no explicit subscription or premium plan flow
- no deep payment workflow beyond settings entry
- no desktop or tablet views
- no moderation/admin back office
