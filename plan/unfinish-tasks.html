4

No rules for money

Answered 14 JulGateway: PayMongo — not wired

Who charges, where it goes, does PickleBallers take a cut, what happens on refunds — same for coaching. Not hard to build; we just didn't know the rules. Real money already moves through the app (tournament entry fees), so we extend that.

WhoJoining their Open Play costsWho keeps it
Any player (no subscription)Free—
Player subscribed as organizer (₱999)They decide — free or paidThey keep it
  • PickleBallers' cut is on the BOOKING only — the court fee the host pays. Already built and live: serviceFeePercent, 7%, on every Booking.
  • PickleBallers takes NO cut of the organizer's join fee. The organizer keeps every peso; the 7% on the booking is the platform's only earning on an Open Play.
  • The organizer subscription is already in the code and purchasable — with zero subscribers.
Worked example

An organizer books an ₱800 court and charges ₱150/head. Six join. → Organizer keeps ₱900, in full. PickleBallers earns 7% of ₱800 ≈ ₱56. Nothing else.

Coach payments — decided 14 Jul
  • Lesson fees go through the app, like a court booking. No more “pay the coach afterwards”.
  • PickleBallers takes 0% of the lesson fee. The coach already paid ₱499 to be a coach.

The revenue model, stated plainly:

MoneyPickleBallers takes
Court booking7%
Organizer's join fee (₱999 sub)0%
Coach's lesson fee (₱499 sub)0%

The subscription is the licence to charge. The platform does not cut into what a partner earns.

The transaction fee — decided 14 Jul

The player pays it. Tax and transaction charges on a court booking or a coaching lesson are borne by the person booking, shown at checkout — not absorbed by PickleBallers, not deducted from the partner. So the 0% cut stays a true 0%.

Refunds — decided 14 Jul

Free-cancellation window: 3 days. Cancel more than 3 days out and the refund is whole — no deduction, whoever cancels. The slot can still be resold. Inside the window, whoever cancels pays the transaction fee.

Who cancelledPlayer gets backWho eats the fee
The player — changed their mindAmount minus the feeThe player
Host / organizer / coach100%, wholeThe canceller — off their payout
Venue, weather, or us100%, wholePickleBallers

The principle: you pay for the damage you cause. A player must never be left worse off by someone else's cancellation than by never having booked.

⚠ Warn the player BEFORE they confirm

When a deduction will come off their refund, the confirmation must say so, with the real numbers, before the button is pressed — “You paid ₱800. You will get back ₱773. A ₱27 transaction fee is deducted.” Then they choose.

No surprises after the fact. A deduction discovered after confirming is how you lose a player for good. If we can't show the exact figure, we don't take the deduction.

⚠ A refund is never free

The gateway does not return the original processing fee when you refund — that money is already spent — and some charge a further refund fee. Every refund costs somebody; the table above only decides who.

Gateway: PayMongo — not set up yet

Do not hardcode a fee percentage. It goes in settings beside serviceFeePercent, and the real numbers get filled in when PayMongo is actually wired. No guessed figures in the code or the UI.

Ang transaction fee — 14 Hul

Ang player ang sasagot. Ang buwis at kaltas sa booking o sa lesson ay sa nagbo-book manggagaling, nakikita sa checkout — hindi sasagutin ng PickleBallers, hindi ikakaltas sa partner. Kaya ang 0% ay tunay ngang 0%.

Refund — 14 Hul

Libreng bintana: 3 araw. Kanselahin nang mahigit 3 araw bago, at buo ang refund — walang kaltas, kahit sino pa. Naibebenta pa ang slot. Sa loob ng bintana, kung sino ang nagkansela, siya ang sasagot sa transaction fee.

Ang batayan: ikaw ang magbabayad sa pinsalang ikaw ang may gawa. Hindi kailanman dapat mas masama ang kalalabasan ng player dahil sa kanselang hindi niya ginawa.

⚠ Sabihan ang player BAGO magkumpirma

Kapag may ikakaltas sa refund, dapat nakasulat, may totoong numero, bago pa mapindot — “Nagbayad ka ng ₱800. Makukuha mo ang ₱773. May ₱27 na transaction fee na ikakaltas.” Siya na ang bahalang pumili.

Walang gulat pagkatapos. Kung hindi maipapakita ang eksaktong halaga, hindi tayo kakaltas.

⚠ Walang libreng refund

Hindi ibinabalik ng gateway ang orihinal na processing fee kapag nag-refund — gastos na yun — at may dagdag pang refund fee ang iba. May gastos ang bawat refund; ang pinagdedesisyunan lang ay sino ang sasagot.

Gateway: PayMongo — hindi pa naka-setup

Huwag i-hardcode ang porsyento. Ilalagay sa settings, katabi ng serviceFeePercent, at ilalagay ang totoong bilang pag na-wire na ang PayMongo. Walang hulang numero sa code o UI.
