# ReClub Pages Map

This file groups the ReClub screenshots into product areas and notes what each group appears to represent.

## Global Observations

- All captured screens are mobile portrait.
- Pickleball is the dominant visible sport.
- The app mixes public-style discovery with member-only club spaces.
- Rankings and community identity are first-class product features.
- Creation tools for clubs, meets, and competitions are unusually prominent.
