# PlayByPoint Theming

## Theme Summary

PlayByPoint uses a clean sports-utility visual system with:

- deep green and dark charcoal foundations
- bright neon-green discovery accents
- white and pale-gray surfaces
- multicolor settings icons
- rounded mobile cards and bottom navigation

The tone feels more operational than playful. It is polished, modern, and facility-focused.

## Visual Character

- Discovery screens lean dark with bright green branding.
- Settings and forms lean white with soft gray sectioning.
- Action buttons are usually white, green, or blue depending on context.
- The icon system uses many colors, especially inside settings lists.

## Typography Direction

The UI looks like a rounded sans-serif with strong readability and slightly friendly forms.

Good fit:

- `Nunito Sans`
- `Avenir Next`
- `DM Sans`

## Component Style

### Cards

- large radii
- very light shadows
- white surfaces

### Inputs

- white fill
- soft neutral border
- rounded corners

### Navigation

- dark bottom bar in discovery
- simple white headers in settings

### CTA behavior

- green is used for discovery/brand emphasis
- blue appears in utility areas and settings
- white primary buttons are used on dark or image-heavy onboarding screens

## Product Mood

- efficient
- club-centric
- professional
- approachable

## Usage Advice

If rebuilding or extending this app, keep:

- the dark-green discovery identity
- the restrained white utility surfaces
- the bright green brand anchor
- the colorful but controlled settings iconography
