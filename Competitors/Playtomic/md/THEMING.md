# Playtomic Theming

## Theme Summary

Playtomic uses a bright, brand-forward visual system with:

- saturated electric blue as the primary brand color
- neon yellow-green as the action-highlight color
- dark navy for premium emphasis
- white surfaces and generous spacing
- rounded pills, chips, and buttons

This is the most branded and consumer-polished of the three app sets.

## Core Palette

- `blue-600` `#355CFF`
- `blue-500` `#4A6BFF`
- `navy-900` `#102638`

- `gold-500` `#D7B35A`

- `text-strong` `#12212D`
- `text-muted` `#72808C`

## Visual Character

- Bold blue header shell on primary tabs
- Very white content areas
- Black/navy text for strong legibility
- Neon accent circles for quick actions
- Premium plan styling uses dark backgrounds and gold accents

## Component Style

### Pills and chips

- heavily used
- rounded full-pill forms
- blue or dark fills for active state

### Buttons

- blue filled CTAs
- dark premium CTAs
- outlined secondary buttons

### Cards

- soft corners
- subtle elevation
- large image-led discovery cards

### Search and filters

- white search bars
- dark filter chips
- lots of chip-based state selection

## Typography Direction

The product looks closest to a clean geometric sans.

Good fit:

- `Inter`
- `Avenir Next`
- `Sora`

## Product Mood

- sporty
- modern
- marketplace-like
- premium-aware

## Usage Advice

If extending this app, preserve:

- the strong blue shell
- the neon accent moments
- the generous whitespace
- the image-led venue discovery style
