*PickleBallers — Update*

We've added a new *"Your Courts"* page that makes managing courts much simpler for venue owners.

*Pick your venue* — a dropdown at the top lets you switch between your venues in one tap.

*All courts at a glance* — every court shows as a clean card with its photo, status (Active / Hidden), sport, surface and features. Shows 2 per row on phone, 3 on tablet, 4 on desktop.

*Add a court* — a quick "Add court" tile sits at the end of the grid.

*Quick actions up top* — "Manage details", "Claim" and "Create" buttons are right in the header.

The original venues page is untouched — nothing was removed. This is a brand-new page alongside it.
